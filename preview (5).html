The Project

One platform, every decision point of a student's career journey.

One Stop Personalized Career and Educational Advisor was built to solve a specific, recurring problem: students choose subjects, colleges, and careers on incomplete information — a relative's opinion, a trending job title, or whichever course a friend picked. This project replaces guesswork with a structured advisory pipeline.

Every module below runs entirely in your browser. Enter your marks, interests, and skills once, and each tool — recommendation engines, dashboards, and the PDF report generator — reads from that same profile to give consistent, explainable suggestions rather than a black-box score.

Capabilities

Everything a career-counselling office does — running client-side.

AI-style Career Matching

Rule-based scoring across interests, subjects and skills surfaces the closest-fit careers, ranked with a match percentage.

Live Course & College Data

A built-in dataset of realistic courses and colleges filters instantly as you change stream, budget, or location.

Skill Gap Visualization

Compares your current skill levels against what your target career demands and lists exactly what to close first.

Auto-generated Roadmaps

A stage-by-stage timeline from "today" to "job-ready," tailored to the career you select.

Analytics Dashboard

One-click PDF Reports

Generate a personalised, printable career report with jsPDF — no server, no sign-up, instant download.

Dark / Light Mode
